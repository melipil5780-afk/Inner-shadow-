1. Who We Are

Wellovie ("we," "us," "our") is a self-guided psychoeducation course operated under the laws of the State of New York, United States. This Privacy Policy governs what information we collect when you purchase or use the Wellovie course (the "Service").

By using the Service, you acknowledge you have read and agree to this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use.

2. What We Collect

2.1 At purchase

When you buy the course through Lemon Squeezy, they collect your payment details and email address to deliver your license key. We do not receive your payment card information. We receive confirmation that a purchase was made and your license key is issued. Lemon Squeezy's privacy practices are governed by their own privacy policy.

2.2 License validation

When you enter your license key to unlock the course, your key is sent to our Cloudflare Worker to verify it against Lemon Squeezy's API. This is a single network call made once per device. No other personal data is transmitted in this call.

2.3 What we do NOT collect

  • Your email address (we never see it)
  • Your name
  • Your password (there is no password — the course uses a license key)
  • Your location or IP address (beyond what Cloudflare sees in transit)
  • Your assessment responses
  • Your reflections, journal entries, or worksheet responses
  • Your progress or streak data
  • Your mood check-in data
  • Any usage analytics

All of the above stays on your device. None of it is sent to any server.

3. How We Use It

The only data we use is your license key, which we verify once to confirm your purchase and unlock the course. We do not use any information for advertising, profiling, or analytics. We do not use your reflection content in any way — we cannot, because we never receive it.

4. Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade any personal information. The only sharing that occurs is:

  • Lemon Squeezy — your license key is validated through their API. They hold your purchase record and email address per their own privacy policy.
  • Cloudflare — our license validation endpoint runs on Cloudflare Workers. Cloudflare may log request metadata (such as IP address) per their standard infrastructure logging practices.
  • Legal requirements — we may disclose information if required by law or valid legal process. Given we hold almost no personal data, there is little to disclose.

5. Data on Your Device

All course content, your progress, reflections, check-ins, and preferences are stored in your browser's local storage. This means:

  • Your data never leaves your device
  • We cannot access it, modify it, or delete it remotely
  • If you clear your browser's site data, your progress will be lost — we cannot restore it
  • If you use multiple devices, your progress does not sync between them

You can clear all local course data at any time through the Settings page within the course.

6. Security

License key validation is transmitted over HTTPS. Because we store no personal data on our servers (your reflections and progress live only on your device), there is no central database of your personal information that could be breached.

The security of your course data depends on the security of your device and browser. We recommend using a modern, up-to-date browser.

7. Your Rights

Because we hold almost no personal data about you, most traditional data rights are exercised directly on your own device. Specifically:

  • Access and deletion of course data: Done through the Settings page within the course, or by clearing your browser's local storage for this site.
  • Purchase records: Managed through Lemon Squeezy. Contact their support to access or delete your purchase information.
  • Questions or requests: Email us at melipil5780@gmail.com. We will respond within 45 days.

8. California Residents — CCPA

California Consumer Privacy Act

If you are a California resident, you have rights under the CCPA. Given the minimal data we collect, most of these rights are satisfied by the nature of our architecture: your personal content never reaches our servers.

We do not sell personal information. We do not share personal information for cross-context behavioural advertising. The only personal information we could theoretically hold is a record that a license key was validated — with no name or email attached on our end.

To exercise any California rights, contact us at melipil5780@gmail.com.

9. Children's Privacy — COPPA

Wellovie is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. Users between 13 and 17 may use Wellovie only with parental consent.

If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, contact us immediately at melipil5780@gmail.com.

10. Sensitive Data and Mental Health Content

Please read this section carefully.

In the course of using Wellovie you may enter personal reflections, emotional responses, and content related to your mental health and personal history. As described above, this content is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to us.

10.1 Not a covered entity under HIPAA

Wellovie is not a healthcare provider, health plan, or healthcare clearinghouse. We are not a "covered entity" under HIPAA. Information you enter is not Protected Health Information under HIPAA. Do not use Wellovie as a substitute for clinical care.

10.2 Crisis situations

Wellovie does not provide crisis intervention. If you are in crisis, please contact the 988 Suicide and Crisis Lifeline (call or text 988), emergency services (911), or a qualified mental health professional.

11. Cookies and Tracking

We do not use advertising cookies. We do not use third-party tracking pixels. We do not engage in cross-site tracking.

The course uses browser local storage (not cookies) to save your progress and preferences on your device. This data does not leave your device.

Cloudflare may set cookies for security and performance purposes on the domains they serve. These are strictly functional.
