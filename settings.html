Reset progress?

This will clear your module progress, streaks, and reflections. Your license key stays — you won't need to re-enter it.

Clear everything?

This removes all data from this device — progress, reflections, streak, and your license key. You'll need to re-enter your key to access the course again.

Use on another device

Your license key works on any device you own. Just open learn.wellovie.com on the new device and enter your key there.
