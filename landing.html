Let's be clear

What this is.
What it isn't.

We'd rather you know exactly what Wellovie is before you sign up than feel misled after.

✓
Wellovie is
  • Psychoeducation — teaching you how emotions and psychology work
  • Content grounded in real psychology — not intuition, not trends
  • A place to develop skills and build self-understanding
  • A complement to therapy, not a replacement for it
  • Appropriate for generally healthy adults doing personal growth work
✕
Wellovie is not
  • Therapy, counselling, or clinical treatment of any kind
  • A crisis service — if you're in crisis, please call or text a helpline
  • A substitute for professional mental health care
  • Appropriate for acute mental health conditions without professional support
  • Staffed by therapists or licensed clinicians
