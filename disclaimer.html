Before we begin

A few honest things you should know about Wellovie — what it is, and what it isn't.

What Wellovie is

✓

Real psychology — explained clearly. Every module teaches you how you actually work emotionally — not inspiration, not trends. The science behind why you are the way you are, made accessible.

✓

Skill-building. You will learn specific, practisable tools — not affirmations. Real skills that change how you relate to your emotions, your patterns, and the people in your life.

✓

A private space. Your worksheets, reflections, and progress are yours alone. We don't share, sell, or analyse your personal data. Ever.

✓

Self-paced and non-judgmental. No right speed. No performance required. Stop, restart, skip, and return as many times as you need.

What Wellovie is not

✗

Not therapy. Wellovie does not provide clinical treatment, diagnosis, or therapeutic care. There is no licensed therapist on the other end of this app.

✗

Not a crisis service. If you are in immediate danger or experiencing a mental health crisis, Wellovie is not the right resource. Please use the services below.

✗

Not a replacement for professional support. If you are managing a serious mental health condition, please work with a qualified clinician. Wellovie can complement that work — it cannot replace it.

🆘 If you're in crisis right now

If you're having thoughts of suicide, self-harm, or are in immediate danger — please reach out. These services are free, confidential, and available right now.

Who this is for

Wellovie is for people in the gap — between struggling alone and sitting in a therapist's office. People who want to understand their emotions, break old patterns, build more genuine connections, and live a life that actually feels like theirs.

The four pathways cover emotional regulation, self-knowledge, connection, and meaningful living. Each one is written to feel human — not clinical.

🔒

Your privacy matters. Your worksheet responses, reflections, and check-ins are stored securely and encrypted. We do not sell your data, share it with advertisers, or use it to profile you. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from Settings.
