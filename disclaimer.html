Before we begin

A few honest things you should know about Wellovie and what it is — and isn't.

What Wellovie is

✓

Evidence-based education. Every module is grounded in real psychology — CBT, attachment theory, ACT, somatic work, self-compassion research. This is the curriculum a therapist might teach, made accessible.

✓

Skill-building. You will learn specific, practisable tools — not inspiration, not affirmations. Real skills that change how you relate to your emotions, your patterns, and the people in your life.

✓

A private space. Your worksheets, reflections, and progress are yours alone. We don't share, sell, or analyse your personal data. Ever.

✓

Self-paced and non-judgmental. There is no right speed. No performance required. You can stop, restart, skip, and return as many times as you need.

What Wellovie is not

✗

Not therapy. Wellovie does not provide clinical treatment, diagnosis, or therapeutic care. There is no licensed therapist on the other end of this app.

✗

Not a crisis service. If you are in immediate danger or experiencing a mental health crisis, Wellovie is not the right resource. Please use the services below.

✗

Not a replacement for professional support. If you are managing a serious mental health condition, please work with a qualified clinician. Wellovie can complement that work — it cannot replace it.

🆘 If you're in crisis right now

If you're having thoughts of suicide, self-harm, or are in immediate danger — please reach out to one of these services. They are free, confidential, and available right now.

988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline Call or text 988 Crisis Text Line Text HOME to 741741 Emergency Services Call 911

Who this is for

Wellovie is designed for people in the gap — between struggling alone and sitting in a therapist's office. People who want to understand their emotions, break old patterns, build more genuine connections, and live a life that actually feels like theirs.

The four pathways cover emotional regulation, self-knowledge, connection, and meaningful living. Each one is grounded in real psychology and written to feel human — not clinical.

Your privacy matters. Your worksheet responses, reflections, and check-ins are stored securely and encrypted. We do not sell your data, share it with advertisers, or use it to profile you. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from Settings.
